How It Works

Upload a video. Get a robot instructor that teaches you the moves.

1

Find a Video

Pick any video of someone dancing, doing yoga, martial arts — anything. YouTube, TikTok, your own recordings all work.

2

Upload It

Drop the video into MoveBot. That's all you need to do — we handle the rest.

3

We Learn the Moves

MoveBot watches the video, figures out every body movement, and teaches them to your Unitree G1 robot.

4

The Robot Practices

Your robot rehearses the moves thousands of times until it can perform them perfectly.

5

Your New Instructor is Ready

The robot performs the moves and teaches them to you step by step. We'll also find related moves to build a full lesson — your personal instructor keeps growing.

Before You Connect

1
Power on your G1 — wait for the status LED to turn solid blue.
2
Same network — make sure your computer and robot are on the same Wi-Fi or wired network.
3
Clear the area — give the robot at least 2m of space in all directions before deploying moves.
4
Stay nearby — always keep the emergency stop within arm's reach during physical deployment.
